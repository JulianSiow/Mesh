Our Story:
Mesh was born of an idea to bring those looking to hire engineers, UX designers, back-end engineers and others, together with the folks looking for positions. All in a non-official casual environment. This would then hopefully result in contacts being made, discussions had, and ultimately, an official interview. Our mission is to bring job seekers and employers together, as well as gave everyone a means to connect, learn, and ultimately grow networks.
